The story about "How did I came to programming?"

The photo made about a few month before I started to learn programming seriously

Some events, that predetermined my future:

  • ~2008 - In middle school, our IT-teacher shows Turbo Pascal to me and my classmates, teaches us the basics and inspiring us to explore the world of programming.
  • 2008 - 2012 - Making a great success in math classes, taking part in dozen competitions.
  • 2014 - Studying in Aviation college, I discover that programming can be used in manufacturing and has even more use in all of our life.
  • 2017 - Diving deeper into the CNC programming I finally find myself enjoying the whole proccess of coding, macking things from scratch and watch how my projects turns out into something valuable.
  • 2018 - Gaining respect from my CNC lecturer, I'm starting to take part in all-Ukrainian competitions and even took some 2nd and 3rd places.
  • February 2019 - Dean of our faculty refuses me to participate in practical courses on a large factory despite all my achievements in CNC programming.
  • May 2019 - After a long confrontation against the dean and failing to get a job or a practicecontained with CNC by myself, I gave up the CNC programming.
  • September 2019 - Working in the outsorcing GIS company I found a great team, which always supported each other and pushed me to give another shot to programing and try myself in learning JavaScript and stuff.
  • December 2019 - Suffering from the terrible working condition, I and my whole team quit the job. Everyone went their own ways with some part-time jobs.
  • January 2021 - Starting to learn JavaScript on freeCodeCamp and other resources like learnJS, soloLearn, etc.
  • April 2021 - Failing few auditions to Trainee position.
  • June 2021 - Finally finished freeCodeCamp JS course and heading to learn more to find myself in this huge and overhelming IT-world.
